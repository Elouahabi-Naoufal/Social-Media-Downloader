Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 24, 2024

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when using our Social Media Downloader Web Application ("Service").

1. Information We Collect

  • URLs: Temporarily processed to download content.
  • Session Data: For tracking downloads during active use (non-personal).
  • Technical Logs: For debugging and system monitoring.
  • Theme Preferences: Stored locally in your browser for better user experience.

We do not collect:

  • Personal identifiable data
  • Account details from social platforms
  • Media contents themselves beyond temporary handling
  • Location data or device information

2. Use of Information

  • Process video/audio/music/media for download
  • Improve speed, quality, and functionality
  • Ensure platform security and prevent abuse
  • Provide download history during your session

3. Cookies and Local Storage

Only functional cookies and local storage are used (e.g., theme preference, temporary session data). We do not use advertising, tracking, or analytics cookies from third parties.

4. Content Types

Downloaded content may include:

  • Music and audio files from various platforms
  • Videos and images, including those featuring people in public content
  • Educational, entertainment, and informational media
  • User-generated content from supported social platforms

We do not store or analyze the content beyond temporary processing required for download.

5. Storage and Automatic Deletion

All files are deleted automatically 30 seconds after download completion. No permanent storage exists on our servers. Your download history is cleared when you close your browser session.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, secure file handling, and automatic cleanup processes to protect your data during the brief processing period.

7. User Rights

You can use the service without registration or account creation. You may stop using it at any time. Since we don't store personal data, there's no data to delete or modify.

8. Third-Party Services

Our service interacts with social media platforms only to retrieve publicly available content. We are not affiliated with Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Facebook, Twitter, or Reddit.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may revise this policy periodically. Continued use of the service means you accept any changes. Major changes will be highlighted on our homepage.

10. International Data Transfers

Since our service operates globally and processes content from international platforms, temporary data processing may occur across different jurisdictions. However, given our 30-second deletion policy, no permanent cross-border data storage occurs.

11. Children's Privacy Protection

We are committed to protecting children's privacy online. Our service does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us immediately. We will take steps to remove such information and terminate the child's access if necessary.

12. GDPR Compliance (European Users)

For users in the European Union, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Your rights include:

  • Right to Access: Request information about data processing (though we store minimal data)
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data (not applicable due to our no-storage policy)
  • Right to Erasure: Request data deletion (automatically fulfilled within 30 seconds)
  • Right to Portability: Receive your data in a structured format (not applicable)
  • Right to Object: Object to data processing (you can stop using the service anytime)

13. CCPA Compliance (California Users)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Know: What personal information we collect and how it's used
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information (automatic in our case)
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Equal service regardless of privacy choices

14. Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities within 72 hours as required by law. However, our automatic deletion policy significantly reduces breach risks since no permanent user data is stored.

15. Technical Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures including:

  • Encryption: All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Secure Processing: Temporary files are processed in isolated environments
  • Access Controls: Restricted access to processing systems
  • Regular Security Audits: Ongoing security assessments and updates
  • Automated Cleanup: Fail-safe mechanisms ensure data deletion

16. Platform-Specific Privacy Considerations

Different social media platforms have varying privacy implications:

  • YouTube: We access only public video data, not user accounts or private information
  • Instagram: Only public posts and stories are accessible for download
  • TikTok: Public videos only, no access to user profiles or private data
  • Facebook: Only publicly shared content, no private posts or user data
  • Twitter/X: Public tweets and media only, no private messages or protected accounts
  • Reddit: Public posts and comments only, no private user information

17. Analytics and Performance Monitoring

We may collect anonymous, aggregated statistics about service usage (such as number of downloads, popular platforms, error rates) to improve performance and reliability. This data cannot be linked to individual users and contains no personal information.

18. Legal Compliance and Law Enforcement

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request. However, given our minimal data collection and automatic deletion policies, there is typically no user data available to disclose.

19. Service Providers and Processors

We may use third-party service providers for hosting, content delivery, or technical support. These providers are contractually bound to protect any data they may temporarily access and cannot use it for their own purposes.

20. Privacy by Design

Our service is built with privacy as a fundamental principle. We collect only what's necessary, process it minimally, and delete it immediately. This "privacy by design" approach ensures your data protection is built into every aspect of our service.

21. Retention Periods

Our data retention schedule is as follows:

  • Downloaded Media Files: Deleted within 30 seconds of download completion
  • Processing Logs: Retained for 7 days for debugging purposes, then automatically deleted
  • Error Logs: Retained for 30 days for service improvement, contain no personal data
  • Session Data: Cleared when browser session ends
  • Temporary URLs: Processed and discarded immediately after use

22. Cross-Border Data Processing

When you download content from international platforms, temporary processing may occur in different countries. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for any cross-border data transfers, though our automatic deletion policy minimizes exposure.

23. Automated Decision Making

Our service uses automated systems to process download requests, detect content types, and manage file cleanup. No automated decisions are made that significantly affect users, and all processing is transparent and predictable.

24. Privacy Impact Assessments

We regularly conduct privacy impact assessments to identify and mitigate potential privacy risks. Our minimal data collection model significantly reduces privacy risks compared to traditional download services.

25. User Control and Transparency

You have complete control over your use of our service. You can see exactly what's being processed (the URL you provide), when it's processed (immediately upon request), and when it's deleted (within 30 seconds). This transparency ensures you're always informed about data handling.

26. Future Privacy Enhancements

We continuously work to enhance privacy protections. Future improvements may include even shorter retention periods, additional encryption measures, and enhanced user control features. Any changes will be communicated through policy updates.

27. Contact Information and Data Protection Officer

For privacy-related questions, concerns, or requests, please contact our Data Protection Officer through our website's contact form or support channels. We respond to all privacy inquiries within 30 days and take your privacy concerns seriously.

Privacy Summary

We collect minimal data, process it temporarily, and delete everything within 30 seconds. Your privacy is protected by design, not just by policy.
